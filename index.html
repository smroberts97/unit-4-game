Crystal Clicker!

Click on the four crystals to match the total given to you.

The number given to you at the beginning of the game is random. Each of the four crystals will be assigned a random value.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number generated. You lose the game if you exceed the random number value.

The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it. Each time the game starts, the game will change the value of the crystal.
