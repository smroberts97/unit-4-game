Crystal Clicker!

Click on crystals to match the randomly generated goal number below.

Each of the four crystals will be assigned a random value, hidden until clicked.

You win the game by matching your score to the goal number. You lose the game if your score exceeds the goal.

Each time the game starts, the game will change the value of the crystal and your goal number.
